Thursday, November 20, 2014, 12:10 P.M.: "Munching with Mozart": "Glimpses". Mary Jane Cope, pianist, with Kathleen Purcell, flute, and Carol Panofsky, oboe, will offer glimpses of music by women composers, featuring compositions of Fanny Mendelssohn, Clara Schumann, Lili Boulanger, Alex Shapiro, and others (including some striking miniatures from composers of the past two decades). Please join us to celebrate this unique but seldom-heard repertoire from the pens of some startlingly talented women!
Santa Cruz County Library, 224 Church St., Santa Cruz, CA. FREE Admission.
